Different Types of Flowers

Sunflowers

Sunflowers are known for their large, bright yellow petals and tall stems. They follow the sun throughout the day.

Roses

Roses are classic flowers that come in many colors and are often associated with love and romance.

Tulips

Tulips bloom in the spring and are known for their cup-shaped flowers. They come in many vibrant colors.
